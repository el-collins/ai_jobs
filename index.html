What is DataAnnotation.tech?

DataAnnotation.tech is a platform where 100k+ experts earn $20–$60+/hr training AI models remotely. It's one of the best entry-level AI income paths — no degree required, just strong reasoning, writing, or coding skills. It serves as a launchpad: annotators build domain expertise and portfolio evidence that opens doors to higher-paying AI engineering roles.

How DataAnnotation.tech works for APF graduates

Step 1Apply on dataannotation.tech — pass skills assessment (writing + reasoning test). No degree required.
Step 2Start with general tasks ($15–20/hr) — writing evaluation, conversation rating, content review.
Step 3Unlock coding category ($35–50/hr) — requires passing a separate programming assessment. Python skills from APF are directly applicable here.
Step 4Use it as a bridge: build documented experience, unlock QA/RLHF roles, then transition to full engineering gigs on Upwork/Indeed.

Key insight for APF curriculum design

DataAnnotation.tech is an immediate income source for APF graduates from Day 1 — even before they land their first engineering contract. Teaching students about RLHF, model evaluation, and prompt quality also makes them better AI engineers AND better-paid annotators.
